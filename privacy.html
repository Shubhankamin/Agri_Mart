Agrimart - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Agrimart, we respect and value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, email, phone number, and shipping address to deliver your orders.
  • Order data: Purchase history, payment details, and invoices to help us manage your transactions.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information to improve site performance.
  • Cookies: Used to remember your preferences and make your experience smoother.

We may also collect optional details when you sign up for newsletters, promotions, or provide feedback.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process, pack, and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • To personalize your shopping experience by recommending relevant products.
  • To improve our website design, speed, and usability.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries quickly.
  • To send important updates such as order confirmations, delivery status, or policy changes.

Marketing emails are sent only if you have opted in, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only in limited cases such as:

  • With trusted partners who provide payment processing, shipping, and IT support.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal proceedings.
  • In the event of a company merger, acquisition, or restructuring.

All partners are required to use your data responsibly and protect it according to legal standards.

4. Data Retention & Security

Your data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Sensitive information such as payment details is encrypted. While we follow industry-standard safeguards, no method of storage or transfer is 100% secure.

We also review our systems regularly to identify and fix vulnerabilities.

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your information at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account or data, where legally possible.
  • Opt-out of promotional messages without affecting service use.
  • Withdraw previously given consent for data processing.

To exercise these rights, simply contact us via email or phone, and we will respond promptly.
