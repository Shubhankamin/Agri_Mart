Introduction

Welcome to Agrimart (“we,” “us,” “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Data We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email, address, phone number
  • Order & transaction details
  • Communications (feedback, support requests)

Information Collected Automatically

We collect data such as:

  • Usage & log data (IP address, pages visited, timestamps)
  • Device & browser info
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

Use of Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain services
  • Communicate with you
  • Personalize content & improve user experience
  • Fraud prevention & security
  • Marketing (with opt-in)

Sharing & Disclosure

We may share data with:

  • Service providers & partners
  • Affiliates or internal entities
  • Legal and regulatory authorities
  • In connection with a business transfer

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, local storage, web beacons, etc. You can manage cookie settings via your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data as needed to fulfill purposes and legal obligations. We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect data, though no system is fully foolproof.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, update your data
  • Request deletion or restriction
  • Object to processing (e.g. marketing)
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or legal minimum age). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If discovered, such data will be deleted.

International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when this occurs.

Third-Party Links & Services

Our site may contain links to third-party websites and services. Their practices and policies are separate; we encourage you to review them.

Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy. We’ll update the “Last Updated” date and notify you of significant changes. Continued use implies acceptance.
